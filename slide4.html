Omega-3 PUFAs “DHA” participates in regulating gut immunity
and in maintaining gut homeostasis1
Role of omega-3 PUFAs “DHA” in improving intestinal immunity:

Influencing the gut microbiota by: 1

  • Modulating the type and the abundance of gut microbes.
  • Altering the levels of proinflammatory mediators.
  • Regulating the levels of short-chain fatty acids or short-chain fatty acid salts.

Omega-3 PUFAs exert their beneficial effects on health, brain functions, and behavior by influencing gut microbiota composition and, thus, gut–brain axis functionality. 2

Reducing the intestinal epithelial cell damage.1

Increasing intracellular mitochondrial activity and cell membrane integrity.1

PUFAs: Polyunsaturated fatty acids; DHA: Docosahexaenoic acid.
